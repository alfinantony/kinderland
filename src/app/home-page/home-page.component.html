KINDERLAND

Job Profile

Kindergarten assistants are responsible for supporting and assisting kindergarten teachers in the classroom. They may help with lesson planning, assist with classroom management, or provide other support to ensure that students receive a quality education. Kindergarten assistants must be able to work well with children of all ages and temperaments.
